AnnealMail post-quantum encryption for your email

named for the physical process used by some quantum computers: quantum annealing

start using smarter encryption today in your browser

What makes AnnealMail special?

  • GPG/PGP encryption is replaced by CodeCrypt
  • CodeCrypt's algorithms are believed to be much more resilient to analysis quantum computers
  • even before quantum computers are developed, you can start encrypting your communication so it's more difficult to decrypt later

An open source experiment

This plugin is based entirely on the popular and user-tested Enigmail plugin for encrypting mail in Thunderbird. If you've used encrypted e-mail before, the experience should be similar.

AnnealMail is not tested as rigorously and is just an experiment. DO NOT USE this for mission-critical things.

This project is not affiliated with Thunderbird, Enigmail, or CodeCrypt. Use at your own risk.
